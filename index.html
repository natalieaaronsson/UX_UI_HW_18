Work

Abilis

Non-Profit UI & Usability Overhaul

In the Research & Define phase, we found one key emotional takeaway and one key practical takeaway to be true: that caretakers are drained, and that seeking help on the Abilis website was too difficult (respectively). Our simplified version of the site centers the information caretakers need while the new “Get Help Now!” form makes seeking help easy and convenient!

Re(mind)ers

Re-imagine the Reminder!

In research, Apple’s “Reminders” app was described as “easy to ignore” and “annoying.” This modern iteration implements more options for notifications, personalization, groupings, and rewards so that users stay engaged and on-track with their goals.
